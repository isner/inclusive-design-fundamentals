Navigation and Consistency

Navigational elements (links, menus) and repeated elements are designed and identified consistently across the site.

Users Affected

Blind

Low Vision

Cognitive

Mobility

Checklist

  • The purpose of each link can be determined by the link text alone

  • At least two ways are provided to navigate through the site or app, such as a table of contents, a site map, or a search feature

  • Elements of menus and other navigation aids that are repeated on multiple pages are presented in the same order across pages

  • Elements that are repeated across pages (like logo images or footer links) are identified consistently across pages
