Visual Information

Color and other visual cues can be used effectively to convey information, but a backup method must be used.

Users Affected

Colorblind

Low Vision

Blind

Cognitive

Checklist

  • All information can be understood when the design is converted to grayscale

  • Links are differentiated from paragraph text by more than just color

  • Instructional text that references size, location, or color also references text-based attributes

  • Focus indicators are visible for everything that's focusable with the keyboard
