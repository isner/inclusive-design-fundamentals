Color Contrast

There should be enough color contrast between text and background for the text to be easily readable.

Users Affected

Colorblind

Low Vision

Elderly

Checklist

  • Color contrast between text of 17pt or lower and the background exceeds 4.5:1

  • Color contrast between text of 18pt or above (or bold text at 14pt or above) and the background exceeds 3:1

  • Best practice: contrast between visual focus indicators and the background exceeds 3:1

  • Best practice: contrast between informative icons and the background exceeds 3:1
