Dynamic Content

Make dynamic content easy to discover and control.

Users Affected

Blind

Low Vision

Cognitive

Checklist

  • Dynamic content is inserted in a location where it can be easily discovered

  • Users can pause, stop, or hide automatically updating content

  • Design annotations specify how focus should behave while interacting with modal components

  • Design annotations specify how assistive technology should respond to content updates in remote parts of the page
